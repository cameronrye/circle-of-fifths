Design Concept

Circular Ring with 12 Key Markers - A modern, geometric logo directly representing the Circle of Fifths musical concept.

Symbolism:

  • 12 Key Markers = All 12 chromatic keys arranged by fifths
  • Outer Ring = The continuous cycle of musical keys
  • Tonic Accent (C) = Emphasized starting point at 12 o'clock
  • Inner Circle = Harmonic center/tonal core
  • Gradient Colors = Modern aesthetic with visual depth
